Life on the Farm

The field

The field is currently covered in lots of brambles, so we need some help clearing it. It's right by the water so there can be a strong wind at times (bear this in mind if you need loose soil/drones for your project).

If you have a strimmer, hedgetrimmers, old carpets or chainsaw it'd be very much appreciated.

Imagine an old concrete shed with no central heating, barely enough electricty and no internet. Look like the place of your nightmares? Welcome to the workspace!

Obviously it needs a bit of TLC - help us make it a place you want to work in.

FAQs

Where is the farm?

The farm is in deepest, darkest Essex near the fine village of Steeple. The better of the two pubs, the Star, is about a five minute drive or an hour's walk away through the fields or the road. The farm is right by the water so there is plenty of sea air. It does get very windy - something to bear in mind.

What facilities are there?

We have basic facilities. There is wifi but it's slow AF. Apparently we're getting real broadband in 2018. There is a place for you to sleep, eat, drink, wash both yourself and your clothes. We have a small 'tractor' and a rickety old trailer with plenty of holes. We also have a paddling pool for when it gets hot ;)

How long can I stay?

As long as you like, as long as a opensource.farm member of staff is down there to look after you.

I think my idea is silly. What do I do?

Who cares? Ask the community for input. This is a space for you to experiment with, so get on it.

Something's come up and I can't make it. What can I do?

:sad-face: Not to worry, just shoot us an email to let us know. If you've booked a spot in the field, you can rebook or we will have to let you plot go. Sigh!

Farm Rules

  • Don't be a dick.
  • No smoking in the house.
  • No swimming in the dark. If you want to swim, let us know. The current is strong.
  • Burn stuff in the designated burn spots. Bear in mind the wind direction so you don't burn everything down.
